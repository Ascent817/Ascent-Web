What Other Alternates Are There?

Other attempts to store renewable energy like Lithium Ions batteries are expensive and ineffecient. Lithium Ion batteries cost almost $300 - 600 per kilowatt hour, while Pumped Hydropower Storage costs only $100 - 200. Additionally, Lithium Ions are damaging to the ecosystem and have a limited capacity.
