What Are The Impacts Of This Technology?

Sustainability is a huge deal, and Hydropower Storage is on the leading edge of that push. Hydropower Storage is the first big step needed to move the world away from fossil fuels and increase reliablilty of renewable energy sources.

Hydropower Storage will also decrease use of Lithium Ion batteries, which as well as being expensive are highly flammable and explosive.
